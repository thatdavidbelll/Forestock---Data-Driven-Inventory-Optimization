# GTM Plan

## Initial Go-To-Market Approach
Recommended launch motion: controlled pilot first, broader public launch later.

## Target Early Audience
- Small retail operators
- Shopify-connected merchants with straightforward catalog structures
- Friendly pilot users willing to give product feedback

## Launch Objectives
- Prove that forecasts and suggestions create practical value
- Validate onboarding and merchant trust
- Gather feedback on recommendation quality and usability
- Build evidence before broader public positioning

## Core Launch Motions
- Direct outreach to pilot merchants
- Hands-on onboarding support
- Capture before/after operational feedback
- Use pilot feedback to improve listing, screenshots, and messaging

## Key Metrics
- Time to first forecast
- Time to first actionable suggestion review
- Forecast completion success rate
- Data sync success rate
- Weekly active operator usage
- Retention of pilot stores after onboarding

## Risks
- Product breadth outpacing onboarding simplicity
- Shopify launch complexity slowing merchant adoption
- Trust gap if recommendation rationale is not clear enough

## Recommendation
Do not lead with a broad public promise until product validation and support playbooks are stronger.
