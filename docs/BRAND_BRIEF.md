# Brand Brief

## Product Positioning
Forestock is a data-driven inventory optimization tool for retail operators who need practical reorder guidance, not black-box automation.

## Core Promise
Help merchants and store managers understand what to restock, when to act, and why a recommendation exists.

## Brand Traits
- Practical
- Trustworthy
- Clear
- Operationally useful
- Human-in-the-loop, not overpromising automation

## Messaging Direction
- Emphasize decision support over autonomous decision-making
- Emphasize clarity and trust in recommendations
- Emphasize fast time-to-value for real store operations

## Visual Direction Notes
- Clean operational dashboard aesthetic
- Confidence through clarity, not hype
- Use inventory / forecasting visuals that feel useful rather than decorative

## Launch Asset Needs
- Product logo direction
- App/store screenshots
- Simple explainers for onboarding and forecast trust
- Listing copy aligned with MVP scope
